Philosophy.

 

Co-sponsor model.

InterPrivates sponsor structure is designed to partner with the firms network of independent industry-specialized private equity and venture capital co-sponsors and operating executives. These exceptional individuals bring deep sector expertise that make our SPACs attractive partners to potential merger targets.
